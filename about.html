About Seda Radoykova

Briefly on what gets Seda out of bed every day

Seda Radoykova is a data scientist with a background in Computational Biology. She is passionate about all things data, but add in a twist of improving patient outcomes through cutting-edge discoveries in healthcare tech. A recent graduate with a pertinent mixture of analytical and communication skills, she is excited to make an impact at the forefront of data-driven solutions.

Seda's intellectual curiosity and fascination with the diversity of living organisms on our planet compelled her to pursue a degree in the Biological Sciences. She has recently attained a first-class MSci in Computational Biology from University College London (UCL), equipping herself with the analytical and software skills to pursue some of the most challenging but stimulating questions from all walks of life (as in, living beings).

BITS OF BIOGRAPHY

Born in Turkey but raised in Bulgaria, Seda comes from a multi-cultural family. In addition to Bulgarian and Turkish, she grew up speaking Russian too, which she learned at school. Seda's passion for connecting with peoples and cultures took her to Duino (Italy), where she attended the United World College of the Adriatic (UWC Adriatic) on a partial merit and need-based scholarship. She flourished in the deliberately diverse environment at the College, where she laid the foundations of many of her passions. Volunteering was one of her favourite aspects of life in Duino; befriending two women with multiple sclerosis expanded her world-view while also pushing her to quickly become conversational in Italian. Having been on the Bulgarian National Debate team before coming to Duino, Seda co-founded the Debate club at the College in her first year.
