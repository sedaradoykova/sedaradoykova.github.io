Welcome to Seda's website

She is excited to show you around

Seda is a data scientist with a background in Computational Biology. She is passionate about all things data, but add in a twist of improving patient outcomes through cutting-edge discoveries in healthcare tech. A recent graduate with a pertinent mixture of analytical and communication skills, she is excited to make an impact at the forefront of data-driven solutions.

Having a long record of volunteering with various non-formal education, health, and human rights organisations, Seda believes in using her capabilities for a force of good in a deliberately diverse and inclusive working environment. She is driven to do work which would improve the lives of people and hopes to inspire more people from under-represented groups in STEM to break the glass ceiling.

Part of this idealism stems from the fact that Seda owes her education to the generosity of merit and need based scholarships for most of her academic career. Her goal is one day to fund two bright university students with the same unreserved support that her sponsor showed for her pursuit of a degree at UCL.
